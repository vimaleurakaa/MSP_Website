Stand up for your community.

What if every believer prayed for their street?


Don’t throw your hands up in despair. Put them together in prayer.


THE
PROBLEM

Darkness and brokenness is embedded in every street corner. Our homes and the very fabric of our lives are torn apart by alcoholism, divorce, substance abuse, debt, depression and every kind of evil. These are not mere statistics. These are not strangers. This is happening in our very own neighborhoods. As Christians we have resigned ourselves to the situation, or worse still become apathetic. We have lapped up the enemy’s lies that the situation is beyond redemption. But instead of looking around you and giving up, we have a higher calling.

We, as believers, don’t just throw our hands up in despair. Instead, we put our hands together in prayer. Because prayer has the power to unleash miracles. Prayer has the power to bind evil. James 4:2 says, You do not have because you do not ask. We are called to stand up for the lost and we are empowered to stand in the gap. Our battle is not against flesh and blood. It’s against the cosmic powers of darkness. And we come against those powers in the Name that is Above Every Other Name.


THE
POWER


THE
SOLUTION

Street Prayer is a simple movement – you as a believer commit to praying for your street, your community. Yet the impact of prayer is far from simple. It’s extraordinary. It’s transformative. It’s supernatural. If every believer takes up that same challenge, your state, your country – and eventually the whole world – is covered in a blanket of prayer. Sign up for Street Prayer – you do your thing and watch God do His. Witness revival in your neighborhood!
